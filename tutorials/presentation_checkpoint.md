# Presentation Checkpoint
## Week 6 in Tutorial
This is a checkpoint to ensure you are on track to have a complete portfolio for submission at the end of semester. The feedback will be provided, and students are expected to ask questions.

## Format
* 10 minutes + feedback + Q&A
* slides
  * Overview
  * Problem definition
  * Solution description
  * State of completion/readiness (what you have done and what you need to do)

Please upload your slides to Canvas. You are welcome to bring your laptop or presentation slides with you on the day of your presentation.
